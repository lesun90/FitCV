\cvsection{RESEARCH AREA}
\cvsubsection{Computational Robotics}
\begin{researchentries}
  \researchentry
    {Combined Task and Motion Planning for Robotic Teams}
    {The purpose of my research is to develop computational methods that would enable robotic teams
to safely and effectively accomplish sophisticated tasks. My research seeks to make it possible for
a human operator to specify the task in a high-level structured language and have the robotic team
automatically plan both the high-level actions and the low-level motions needed to accomplish the
task and coordinating their plans when necessary. Such research could have a significant impact in
enhancing automation in transportation, exploration, and search-and-rescue missions.}
